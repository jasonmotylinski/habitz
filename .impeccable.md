# Habitz Design Context

## Target audience
Household users (primarily Courtney) who check in daily on mobile. Wellness + budget tracking — personal, not corporate.

## Use cases
- Glance at today's habits (done / not done)
- Check weekly budget progress at a glance
- Log food, workouts, fasting

## Brand personality
Warm, cozy, personal. Like a home wellness journal, not a fitness app. Dark background with warm accent colors. Feels settled and calm, not urgent or gamified.

## Design system
- Background: #0f1117 (very dark warm-tinted black)
- Surface: #1a1d27
- Text: #e8eaf0 (warm off-white)
- Text muted: #8b8fa8
- Accent: #4a6cf7 (blue)
- Green: #22c55e, Amber: #f59e0b, Red: #ef4444
- Body font: DM Sans (budget app), system sans elsewhere
- Display font: DM Serif Display (budget amounts)
- Radius: 12px, mobile-first, PWA
